Talentia graphical charter relevant extraction notes:

Logo:
- Main Talentia logo has protected area requirements.
- Minimum size references include 33 mm and preferred 45 mm.
- For digital use below minimum size, responsive logo and then icon version are used.
- White logo can be used on blue background.
- White logo can be used on pink background.
- Forbidden logo uses:
  - do not create blue or pink monochrome version
  - do not apply contour
  - do not change logotype color
  - do not rotate
  - do not add shadow/effects
  - do not use logo on non-identity-color backgrounds
  - do not place logo on unreadable background
  - respect protection area

Primary colors:
- Dark blue: #24135F
- Pink: #D0006F
- Grey: #B1B3B3

Secondary colors:
- Violet: #422990
- Turquoise: #2DCCD3

Color segmentation:
- Violet is main secondary color used in documents.
- Pink is reserved for HR documents.
- Turquoise is reserved for financial documents.
- General documents use brand colors plus violet.
- Finance documents may use turquoise.

Typography:
- Brand identity font: Campton
- Office documents font: Arial / Arial Unicode MS
- For office documents prefer Arial.

Use the output for PowerPoint/Office rendering and validation.
